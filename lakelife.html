How routes get built
Every night, the scheduler takes tomorrow's confirmed jobs and:
1 · ClusterGroup by lake, then shore segment
2 · SequenceOrder stops in drive direction
3 · BalanceCap at crew daily capacity
4 · PairPier + lift crews land same address
5 · PushMap link texted to each crew, 8p
Mowing and cleaning are the daily volume game — dense same-shore loops are what let one GreenEdge crew clear 8+ lawns a day. New signups slot into the nearest existing loop automatically.
